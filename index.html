Easy lookup web accessibility guidelines

We want the process of making an accessible website easier. These guidelines contain all of the criteria that we feel suffice to ensure a website can be used by every kind of user. They incorporate much of WCAG 2.0, some of RNIB's Surf Right standard and some of our own too.

Search by priority level, department or keyword and get a succinct explanation of the relevant criteria. Comments and feedback welcome.
